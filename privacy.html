Privacy Policy

1.2 About Us

Natter LLC (doing business as "RookFounder") is a Wyoming-registered Limited Liability Company operating the RookFounder automation platform. For privacy-related inquiries, contact: office@rookfounder.com.

1.3 Scope

This policy applies to all personal data processed through RookFounder's platform, including web applications, mobile apps, AI automation tools, third-party integrations, and associated services, whether accessed via subscription, free trial, or promotional offerings.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Provided Data

When using RookFounder, you may provide: account registration details (name, email, business information), payment credentials (processed via Stripe), communication content (SMS/email messages), and user-generated configurations (workflows, automation rules).

2.2 Automatic Collection

We automatically collect: IP addresses, device/browser characteristics, usage patterns (session duration, feature interaction), cookie data, and system logs for security and operational purposes.

2.3 Third-Party Data

We may receive information from integrated services you connect (e.g., Google Calendar events, Stripe payment confirmations, or CRM contact imports).

2.4 AI Data

Platform AI tools process: chat transcripts, command histories, and anonymized interaction patterns to improve responses. This data is decoupled from personal identifiers after 30 days of inactivity.

Your personal data will not be shared with any third parties.
