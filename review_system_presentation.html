How Our System Works

Well, first of all — we don't just blast review requests to everyone. Instead, we reach out to every customer and first check how likely they would rate you with a 5-star review.

Handling Unhappy Customers

If someone had a bad experience, and their response contains anything less than 5 — less than great — we ask them for advice instead of a review.

People love giving advice. And at that point, they feel understood, and believe their feedback has been addressed — which it has. Sometimes it's even very useful advice.

They don't stay upset, and more importantly, they don't leave a bad public review.

"No-Based" Questions

Everyone who had a good experience gets invited to leave a review using "no-based" questions — a method proven to get far higher response rates than the traditional way.

❌ Traditional

"Hey, would you leave us a review?"

✅ Our Method

"Would it be too much trouble to leave us a review?"

The Psychology

Because when people say "yes," they feel open and exposed — like someone tricked them into doing something they wouldn't normally do.

But if they say "no," they feel safe and secure.

This taps into human psychology and gets far more people to actually leave you a good review.
